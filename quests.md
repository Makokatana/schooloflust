# Mom

### Mom the Bum
- Talk to Mom in the morning on a weekday and give her $100 (+1 Romance)

<br>
<br>

### Mom No Longer a Bum
**Requirements:**  
- Complete the sidequest: Mom the Bum
- Complete the sidequest: Finding a Disguise

**Walkthrough:**  
- Go to Mom's room and read the diary on her nightstand
- When it's evening go to town and enter the Bunny Bar

# Natalie

### Natalie's Peeping Tom
**Requirements:**  
- Quest becomes available on 9/11

**Walkthrough:**  
- Outside your house -> Talk to Neighbor
- After a day passes go to Natalie's room in the morning
- Shopping mall -> Buy drill (-$30) -> Bed -> Peephole
- Look inside the peephole
- Shopping mall -> Buy cami top (-$50)
- Talk to the neighbor
- Natalie route:
  - ?
- Violet route:
  - Violet -> Spend time in cafe (-$5) (+2 Charm) (+1 Romance)
  - Cherish this moment (+1 Romance)
- Talk to the neighbor

<br>
<br>

### Hypno Natalie #1
**Requirements:**  
- Quest becomes available on 9/9
- 3 Knowledge

**Walkthrough:**  
- Library -> Search bookcase (Received magic book volume 1)
- Your room -> Inventory -> Magic book volume 1
- Quest becomes advanceable on 9/12
- Complete objective #6 in the sidequest: Natalie's Peeping Tom
- Natalie's room -> Natalie -> Hypnotise

<br>
<br>

### Hypno Natalie #2
**Requirements:**  
- Complete the sidequest: Hypno Natalie #1

#2 - Go back and hypnotize Natalie again.

# Violet

### Harassing the Harasser
**Requirements:**  
- Complete the sidequest: Job Agency

**Walkthrough:**  
- Cafe -> Violet -> Talk -> Optional: Ask the three questions -> That's all.
- Enter the cafe when it's evening
- Go back inside -> Violet -> Talk

<br>
<br>

### Call the Wambulance!
**Requirements:**  
- Complete the sidequest: Harassing the Harasser

**Walkthrough:**  
- In the morning go outside to the playground
- Tell her it's you (+1 Romance)

<br>
<br>

### Rescue the Wambulance!
**Requirements:**  
- Complete the sidequest: Call the Wambulance!
- 3 Charm

**Walkthrough:**  
- In the morning at the playground talk to Violet
- Help Session #1
- In the northeast part of town go to the food stall and buy cooking oil (-$5)
- Help Session #2 -> "I'm admiring your butt" (+1 Romance)
- Violet romance level = 2
- Help Session #3 -> ?
- incomplete

**Walkthrough:**  
- Warning: You won't be able to hang out with Violet at the cafe anymore after completing this quest
